A Restauranter's Palette

My sweet spice:

Born and raised in the Lone Star State, Lily Tran embarked on her culinary journey inspired by her family's rich food culture. With roots from the East, Tran developed
a deep love for Asian cuisine while also exploring the diverse flavors of America's culinary melting pot. Despite lacking formal training and professional experience,
Tran's passion for the culinary arts drove her to be self-taught, utilizing resources like social media and the internet to learn and experiment with new recipes.
From Tex-Mex to Asian stir-fries and creamy Italian pastas, each culinary venture was a chance for Tran to hone her skills through perseverance and practice.
Supported by her loved ones, Tran is proud to pursue her passion for cooking and is determined to turn it into a fulfilling career where she can cherish every moment.
