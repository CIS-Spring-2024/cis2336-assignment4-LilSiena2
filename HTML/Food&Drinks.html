------------ Menu -------------

Sandwiches:

Classic BLT:

Bacon, lettuce, and tomato with mayonnaise on toasted bread.

Turkey Club:

Sliced turkey, bacon, lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise on three slices of toasted bread, stacked in layers.

Egg Salad:

Hard-boiled eggs mixed with mayonnaise, mustard, and seasonings, served on bread or a roll with lettuce.

Caprese:

Fresh mozzarella, tomato slices, basil leaves, and balsamic glaze on toasted ciabatta or baguette.

Chicken Salad:

Shredded chicken mixed with mayonnaise, celery, onions, and seasonings, served on bread or a croissant with lettuce.

Tuna Melt:

Tuna salad with melted cheese on toasted bread or a roll, optionally with tomato slices.

Vegetarian Panini:

Grilled vegetables (such as zucchini, bell peppers, and onions) with pesto and mozzarella cheese pressed between slices of bread.


Coffee:

Drip Coffee:

Freshly brewed coffee made by pouring hot water over ground coffee beans, served black or with milk and sugar. (Regular/Decaf)

Espresso:

A shot of concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Americano:

Espresso diluted with hot water, resulting in a milder coffee flavor.

Cafe Latte:

Espresso mixed with steamed milk, typically with a small amount of foam on top.

Cappuccino:

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, creating a creamy and frothy texture.

Cafe Mocha:

Espresso mixed with chocolate syrup or cocoa powder, steamed milk, and topped with whipped cream.

Iced Coffee:

Chilled brewed coffee served over ice, optionally sweetened and with milk or cream.

Cold Brew Coffee:

Smooth and rich coffee brewed with cold water over an extended period, served over ice, syrup and sweetners available


Sweet Treats:

Tiramisu:

Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa powder with a flavor of espresso.

Creme Brulee:

French dessert consisting of rich custard topped with a layer of caramelized sugar.

Pastries:

Croissants, Muffins, Scones, Baklava, Danish pastries

Lemon Tart:

a buttery tart crust filled with tangy lemon curd, garnished with whipped cream or fresh berries.

Fruit Tart:

buttery shortcrust pastry filled with cream and garnished with fresh fruit

Cakes:

Vanilla, Chocolate, Fruit, Lemon, Red Velvet

Ice Cream:

Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry, Butter Pecan, Mint Chocolate, Mango Sorbet

unique ice cream flavor made weekly - please ask staff for more details
